Broiler Chicken Farming

Category: Poultry | Output: Meat Production

Modern, high-yield methods for commercial chicken meat production in controlled environments.

üí∞ Value Proposition

Broilers offer the quickest return on investment in animal farming, with market weight achieved in 6-9 weeks. High global demand for white meat makes it a staple market product.

üìò Step-by-Step Production Guide

  1. **Pre-Stock Preparation:** Sanitize the entire house, lay fresh litter (shavings), and set up brooder rings with heat lamps and feeders.
  2. **Brooding (Day 1 - Week 2):** Maintain strict temperature control (95¬∞F initially). Provide easy access to water and specialized starter feed. Monitor chick health frequently.
  3. **Growth Phase (Week 3 - 5):** Remove brooder rings. Increase feeder space. Transition to grower feed. Ensure continuous, excellent ventilation to prevent respiratory issues.
  4. **Finisher Phase (Week 6 - 9):** Switch to finisher feed. Reduce lighting to encourage eating and resting. Monitor weight gain to hit target market size.
  5. **Harvest/Processing:** Follow ethical and hygienic processing standards. Thoroughly clean and disinfect the house before the next batch arrives.
