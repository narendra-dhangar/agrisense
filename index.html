Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main difference between an Arid and Tropical guide? +

Arid guides focus on **water efficiency, drought-tolerant breeds, and temperature management** during extreme heat. Tropical guides prioritize **disease and parasite control** due to humidity, and housing to promote air flow.

Are these guides suitable for both small-scale and commercial farms? +

Yes, our guides are structured to include sections on **minimal resource requirements** for beginners and **scalable infrastructure designs** for commercial operations.

How often are the best practices updated? +

Our content team updates key guides **quarterly** or immediately following significant agricultural regulatory changes or scientific breakthroughs in animal husbandry.
