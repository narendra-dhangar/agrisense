Tilapia Pond Systems (Warm Water)

Category: Aquaculture | Output: Affordable Protein Source

Often called the 'aquatic chicken' due to their fast growth and resilience, Tilapia thrive in controlled pond systems in warmer climates.

Tilapia reproduce easily and grow quickly, offering high stocking density potential. They convert feed efficiently, making them an excellent source of inexpensive protein for local consumption.
